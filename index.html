Identification of People Using Different Mosquito Prevention Techniques

  • Public Health Focus: Addresses the critical challenge of mosquito-borne diseases like dengue, malaria, and Zika, which threaten communities worldwide.
  • Community Survey: Engages local residents to identify their mosquito prevention methods, including repellents, nets, and environmental controls.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Analyzes the effectiveness and accessibility of various techniques to inform better practices.
  • Awareness & Education: Raises community awareness about sustainable prevention strategies to reduce health risks.
  • Impactful Outcomes: Aims to empower residents with knowledge and resources, fostering a healthier, safer environment.
