Conclusion

  • Key Findings: The survey revealed diverse prevention methods, with 64% removing stagnant water and 42% using nets, but gaps in consistent repellent use.
  • Community Impact: Raised awareness of effective practices, empowering residents to reduce mosquito-borne disease risks.
  • Government Role: 56% observed government efforts, indicating a need for stronger local campaigns and resource distribution.
  • Future Steps: Promote eco-friendly solutions, enhance education, and collaborate with communities for sustainable prevention.
