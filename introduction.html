Introduction

  • Global Health Challenge: Mosquito-borne diseases like dengue, malaria, and Zika affect millions, causing significant illness and economic strain in tropical regions.
  • Community Engagement: Surveys local residents to document their use of prevention methods, such as repellents, nets, and environmental controls.
  • Knowledge Gaps: Identifies barriers to effective prevention, including access to resources and awareness of best practices.
  • Sustainable Solutions: Proposes practical, eco-friendly strategies to enhance community protection against mosquitoes.
  • Empowering Change: Educates and equips residents to reduce health risks, fostering a safer, healthier community.
